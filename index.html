About Me

I am a first-year PhD student supervised by prof. Dawei Zhou in the Computer Science Department at Virginia Tech. I received my Master's degree in Biostatistics supervised by prof. Yun Li at UNC-Chapel Hill in 2024, and my Bachelor's degree in Statistics and English Literature and Linguistics at Zhejiang University in 2022.

My research interests are broadly in Machine Learning and Data Mining. My enthusiasm is to address challenges in open-world machine learning, including developing theories and frameworks to better understand LLM behaviors.
